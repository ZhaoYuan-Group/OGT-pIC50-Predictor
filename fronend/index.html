About OGT pIC50 Predictor

What is OGT?

O-GlcNAc transferase (OGT) is an enzyme that catalyzes the addition of O-linked β-N-acetylglucosamine (O-GlcNAc) to serine or threonine residues of many nuclear and cytoplasmic proteins. It plays a crucial role in various cellular processes and has been implicated in several diseases, making it an important therapeutic target.

About pIC50 Prediction

The pIC50 value is a negative logarithmic measure of the potency of a substance in inhibiting a specific biological or biochemical function. It is calculated as -log10(IC50), where IC50 is the concentration of a substance required to inhibit a given biological process by 50%.

Our predictor uses machine learning models trained on known OGT inhibitors to predict pIC50 values for new compounds, helping researchers prioritize candidates for further study.

OGT pIC50 prediction model flow chart

Models Used

The tool incorporates several predictive models based on different molecular descriptors:

  • rdkit_2d_normalizedModel - Uses normalized 2D molecular descriptors from RDKit
  • DaylightModel - Based on Daylight fingerprints
  • ErGModel - Uses ErG fingerprints
  • MorganModel - Based on Morgan circular fingerprints
